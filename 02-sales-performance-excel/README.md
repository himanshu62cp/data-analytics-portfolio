# Sales Performance & Revenue Insights (Excel Case Study)

## Business Problem
Understanding sales performance across regions, products, and time periods is critical for making informed business decisions.  
This project analyzes sales data to identify revenue drivers, high-performing segments, and areas for improvement using **Microsoft Excel**.

---

## Objective
- Analyze overall sales and revenue trends
- Compare performance across products and regions
- Identify high-value and underperforming segments
- Provide actionable business insights using Excel

# Sales Performance & Revenue Insights (Excel Case Study)

## Business Problem
Understanding sales performance across regions, products, and time periods is critical for making informed business decisions.  
This project analyzes sales data to identify revenue drivers, high-performing segments, and areas for improvement using **Microsoft Excel**.

---

## Objective
- Analyze overall sales and revenue trends
- Compare performance across products and regions
- Identify high-value and underperforming segments
- Provide actionable business insights using Excel

---

## Key Insights
- Revenue analysis shows that sales are concentrated in specific regions, with certain regions contributing a majority of total revenue.
- Product-level analysis indicates that **higher-priced products such as laptops** drive a significant share of total revenue.
- Sales performance varies across salespeople, highlighting top performers who consistently generate higher revenue.
- A small number of high-value orders contribute disproportionately to total sales, indicating opportunities for targeted sales strategies.

---

## Business Recommendations
- Focus sales and marketing efforts on **high-performing regions and products** to maximize revenue.
- Replicate best practices from **top-performing salespeople** across the sales team.
- Encourage upselling of high-value products to increase average order value.
